Contemporary Dance & Fitness Studio in 360-Degrees!

A dance studio seemed like the perfect place for my first 360 video project.  Join 3 very different classes in this beloved studio.  Be sure to view this in a browser that supports 360 video (Safari doesn't).

Act 46 Documentary

Heralded as "clear, compassionate filmmaking, illuminating an extremely complex subject," this film explores the status and possible future of Vermont's long tradition of local democracy.

Visit the Maple Corner Store

Small  general stores can't compete with larger chains on price, so why should we  shop in them?  This short promotional piece offers up a few reasons to love the Maple Corner Store/ Whammy Bar in Calais, VT.

I co-produced this video overview for the Vermont Council on Rural Development.  The Community Visit program gets citizens engaged in working for their communities and connects them to the resources they need to be successful.
